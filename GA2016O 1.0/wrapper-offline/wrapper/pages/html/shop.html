My content and what I can do with it

Can I submit a mod?

Absolutely! Just DM killercaillou657#6449 on Discord and I'll look into adding your mod into the Wrapper Shop!

Requirements

You MUST include a picture that is 387x218. My DM's are NOT support or help. Ask Wrapper Central. Also include a .zip file with instructions. Spamming my DM's will result in an instant termination. Repeatedly asking me and annoying me to do it now will also result in a termination.

Can I remove my content?

Yes you can! I'd like to have a big library of mods all in one place, but you can ask to remove your mods from here.

Can I update my content?

Sure! If you have to remove something or make some changes, you can update your content! Just make sure to send me a .zip file and I'll put it up here!
